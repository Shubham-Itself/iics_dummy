Recruitment & hiring

IICS is India’s first creative incubator focused on Media, Entertainment, and Creative Technology. Unlike traditional colleges, we blend skill-based training with real industry exposure, offering personalized mentorship, global trips, and project-based learning.

We offer degree and certificate programs in areas like Animation & Gaming, Digital Content Creation, Sound Design, Event Management, Costume Design, and more—designed with direct input from industry leaders.

Yes, all degree programs are offered in collaboration with reputed universities like Jain Online and are aligned with NEP & NCrF guidelines. We are backed by NSDC and powered by the Media & Entertainment Skills Council (MESC).

Any student who has completed 12th grade (or equivalent) from a recognized board and has a passion for creativity, storytelling, or media can apply. No prior experience is needed for most programs.

Our model is flexible—3 hours a day, 3 days a week. Students receive masterclasses, internship opportunities, real-time feedback from top professionals, and international exposure to events like Cannes, MIPCOM, and SIGGRAPH.

Employee development programs

Absolutely. Every student gets access to live projects, internships, creative labs, and a guaranteed 3-month paid apprenticeship with media studios, production houses, or agencies.

Depending on your course, you can become a game designer, animator, filmmaker, sound engineer, content creator, event producer, stylist, digital strategist, or launch your own creative startup.

Yes, we offer need-based scholarships and easy EMI options. Select programs also come with assured stipends between ₹35K–₹50K, issued at the time of registration.

Yes, we welcome students from across India and abroad. Our campuses are well-equipped with hostel facilities and a global-friendly learning environment.

You can apply online through our website. Once you fill the form, our admissions team will reach out for an entrance interview and portfolio (if required), followed by offer letter and onboarding.
